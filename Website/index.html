ABOUT

Vortice Alpha - VOAL is a sci-fi, first person shooter game set in a space system many billions of miles away from Earth. You are a captured human "working" for the VOAL army but you learn the truth when you have to fight rebel humans.

Many armies in this universe clash to control many planets but all you can do is mindlessy fight for the VOAL army as your mind has been taught that you are one of the last humans alive and that the VOAL have protected the remaining humans.

If this sounds interesting then I recommend you click the download button and learn the truth yourselve. The faith of humanity is counting on you.
